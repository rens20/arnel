In the realm of ethereal thoughts, where consciousness lies
A universe unexplored, under the mind's skies.
In the labyrinth of introspection, one might lose their way,

Yet in this complexity, our deepest fears lay.

Through the kaleidoscope of emotions, we find our truth,
In the crucible of time, we lose our youth.

Yet in the symphony of life, each note we play,
Echoes in the cosmos, in a profound ballet.
